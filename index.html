WORDLE AI

HOW TO USE

This is a tool that recommends optimal guesses for the daily word game Wordle.

Guess the word shown. Click on the tiles to make them match the colors of the tiles on Wordle. Then click NEXT GUESS or press ENTER and repeat.

This tool can guess the WORDLE in an average of 3.42 guesses and a maximum of 5 guesses, which was shown to be optimal by Alex Selby.

The AI tries first to maximize the number of unique tile colorings that can be given for a guess, and second to minimize the variance of the number of possibilities that could remain after making the guess.

The AI is trained to only guess words in the Wordle dictionary.

SETTINGS

Dark Mode
Color Blind Mode
High contrast colors
